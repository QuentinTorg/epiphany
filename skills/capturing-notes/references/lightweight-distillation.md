# Lightweight Distillation

Lightweight distillation happens immediately after raw capture.

What to do:

- update `## Current Summary`
- update `## Open Questions`
- update `## Candidate Action Items`
- update `## Distillation Notes`
- identify obvious topic and entity references
- keep the thread pending for later deep reconciliation

What not to do:

- do not create broad topic changes here
- do not close the thread here
- do not treat lightweight distillation as full reconciliation

This is a thread-local synthesis pass, not a workspace-wide one.
